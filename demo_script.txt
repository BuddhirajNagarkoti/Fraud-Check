FRAUD CHECK — Demo Script (~3:30 minutes)
============================================

OPENING (0:00 - 0:15)
----------------------
[Show the app landing screen on mobile]

"Fraud Check is a voice-first consumer rights assistant for Nepal, powered by
Google Gemini Live. It helps everyday consumers identify fraud, understand their
legal rights, and take action — all through natural conversation and their phone
camera."


SCENE 1: Voice Conversation (0:15 - 0:50)
------------------------------------------
[Tap the mic orb to start a voice session]

YOU SAY: "I bought a packet of chips from a local shop and the shopkeeper
charged me 50 rupees more than the MRP printed on the packet. Is that legal?"

[Let Gemini respond with Consumer Protection Act citation]

YOU SAY: "What can I do about it?"

[Let Gemini explain complaint filing process]


SCENE 2: Live Camera — Overpriced Receipt (0:50 - 1:40)
--------------------------------------------------------
[Prepare a fake receipt: MRP Rs. 150, Charged Rs. 220]
[Tap "Live Session" button to open camera + voice]

YOU SAY: "I have the receipt here, can you look at it?"

[Hold receipt to camera]
[Let Gemini read and identify the overcharge]

YOU SAY: "Is this a violation?"

[Let Gemini confirm and cite the law]
[Tap the camera feed to capture as evidence]


SCENE 3: Live Camera — Expired Product (1:40 - 2:30)
-----------------------------------------------------
[Prepare a product with a handwritten label showing expiry: 2025-12-01]

YOU SAY: "Now look at this product I bought yesterday"

[Hold the label to camera]
[Let Gemini read the expiry date and flag it]

YOU SAY: "Where do I report this?"

[Let Gemini provide complaint guidance]
[Tap to capture evidence]


SCENE 4: Nepali Language (2:30 - 3:00)
---------------------------------------
[Still in live session or start new voice session]

YOU SAY (in Nepali): "Maile euta saman kine tara tyo kharab niskiyo, ma ke
garna sakchu?"
(Translation: I bought a product but it turned out defective, what can I do?)

[Let Gemini respond in Nepali]
[Shows multilingual capability]


CLOSING (3:00 - 3:30)
----------------------
[Show the evidence panel with captured photos]
[Show the violations detected sidebar]

"Fraud Check combines real-time voice, live camera analysis, and Nepal's
consumer protection laws into one accessible tool. It's built with Gemini Live
API, deployed on Google Cloud Run, and works as a PWA on any phone. Thank you."


PROPS TO PREPARE
================
1. Fake Receipt
   - Item: Chips
   - MRP: Rs. 150
   - Amount Charged: Rs. 220
   - Add a shop name and date
   - Handwrite it on paper (looks more authentic)

2. Expired Product Label
   - Product name (anything)
   - Mfg Date: 2025-06-01
   - Exp Date: 2025-12-01
   - Add a batch number
   - Handwrite on paper or stick on a product


TIPS
====
- Practice once before recording so you know Gemini's response timing
- Keep your voice natural, not scripted-sounding
- If Gemini says something unexpected, roll with it — judges like authentic demos
- Record on your phone with screen recording (not a separate camera filming screen)
